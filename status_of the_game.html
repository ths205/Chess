Status of chess

Checkmate

Is when a player's king is threatened with a check and there is no way for the king to get out of being captured. The player of the king who is checkmated is the one who loses the game. In some cases ,before checkmate can occur ,the player who is losing and checkmate is inevitable will resign befire it could occur. Check

Is when the player's king is under attack with a check but is able to get out of the check. A king can get out of check in three ways: The first is by moving one space in any direction that would get the king out of check. The second is for another piece to interpose itself between the attacking piece and the king. The third would be to capture the attacking. Stalemate

Is when it is player's turn to move the king,which is not in check, but cannot because there are no legal moves the king could make. When the game ends in a stalemate, it means that the game is a draw. Return to home page
