    Chess is a game for two players that involves a chessboard where the chess pieces are moved. At the beginning of the game, each player has a king ,queen, two rooks, two bishops, and eight pawns. Each piece moves differently depending on the piece that is being moved. The primary purpose in the game is to checkmate the king using skill and strategy.
